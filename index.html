Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Academic Skill Booster.

What is this bootcamp for?

+

Our Academic Skill Booster is designed specifically for Class 10 students preparing for board exams or wanting to strengthen their understanding of core concepts. It's suitable for students at all levels - whether you're struggling with fundamentals or just want to optimize your performance for top scores. The program adapts to your current skill level and learning pace.

What subjects are covered?

+

The bootcamp focuses on Mathematics, Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology), and English - the core subjects for Class 10 board exams. You can choose to focus on one subject area or take a multi-subject approach based on your needs. Our diagnostic test will help identify which subjects would benefit most from intensive revision.

How much time commitment is required?

+

The intensive program requires about 3-4 hours per day for 7 days. This includes 1-2 hours of live sessions, 1 hour of guided practice, and 1 hour of self-paced learning. We've designed it to fit into a student's schedule without overwhelming them, with breaks built in for optimal learning retention.

What if I'm not satisfied with the program?

+

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you complete the program (attend all sessions and complete the assignments) and don't feel it met your expectations, we'll provide a full refund. Our goal is your academic success, not just taking your money. In our 3 years of operation, we've had less than a 2% refund rate.

How soon can I see results?

+

Most students report noticeable improvement within the first 3 days of the program as they start applying our learning techniques. By the end of the week, average score improvements range from 20-40% on practice tests. However, maximum benefits are typically seen 2-3 weeks after completing the bootcamp as students continue applying what they've learned.
